  • Swami Rama Himalayan University

    Sri Ramachandra Institute of Higher Education and Research, established in 1985 under the Sri Ramachandra Educational & Health Trust, has evolved into a "Deemed to be University" since September 1994. Accredited by NAAC with A++ Grade and graded by UGC as Category I University, it is located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. The institution offers a wide range of medical programs, including undergraduate MBBS with an annual intake of 250 students, as well as postgraduate and super specialty courses. With a sprawling 150-acre campus, the university is dedicated to providing an experiential learning environment and has established alliances with institutions such as the University of Hong Kong. Committed to excellence in medical education, research, and patient care, Sri Ramachandra Institute of Higher Education and Research continues to make significant contributions to the field of healthcare.
