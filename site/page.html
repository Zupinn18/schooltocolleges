NEET UG 2024 Counselling Process Overview

The NEET UG 2024 counselling process commences with fundamental registration and choice filling/locking with the relevant counseling authorities, followed by seat allotment and subsequent reporting. Candidates must fulfill the eligibility criteria and actively participate in each stage of the process to secure their desired seats in medical.

1

Registration

Based on their eligibility, participants can register with the Medical Counselling Committee (MCC). The registration process involves fulfilling the eligibility criteria and submitting the necessary documents for verification.

2

Choice filling

Based on their marks and preferences, candidates can select multiple colleges and lock down their choices.

3

Seat allotment

Authorities allocate seats based on merit and candidates' preferences, ensuring a fair and personalized selection process for participants.

4

Reporting

Report to the assigned medical institute with necessary documents, DD, and fulfill the joining formalities to complete the process.
